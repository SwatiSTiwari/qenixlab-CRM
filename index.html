Executive Summary

This proposal outlines the development, deployment, and management of a fully integrated Smart AI Sales Agent via WhatsApp combined with a robust In-House CRM System designed specifically for Voyx India's B2B distribution business.

This system will enable Voyx India to automate lead capture, provide instant AI-driven product responses, intelligently qualify and route dealers based on location, and seamlessly manage the entire purchase and dispatch lifecycle within a custom CRM.

Detailed Working Flow of the AI Sales Agent (Version 1)

The AI Sales Agent is not just a chatbot. It is designed to behave like a trained sales executive who works 24 hours a day without getting tired. Let us understand its working step by step in very simple terms.

1) Customer Sends a Message on WhatsApp

Whenever a customer sends a message like "Do you have charging cables?", "I want mobile accessories", "Send your price list", or "I want to become a dealer", the AI immediately replies within seconds.

It greets the customer politely and asks basic questions such as:

  • What product are you looking for?
  • What is your location / Pin Code?
  • Are you buying for shop or personal use?
  • Approximate quantity required?

This helps the AI understand whether the person is serious and whether they are a retailer or not.

2) Location & Dealer Protection Logic (Very Important for B2B)

Since this is a distribution business, we must protect existing dealers. So after the customer gives their Pin Code, the AI checks: Is there already a registered dealer in that area? Is there any dealer within a 50 km radius? Now two situations happen:

  • Situation A (Dealer Already Exists): The AI politely informs the customer that there is an authorized dealer nearby, shares the product catalog for reference only, offers contact details of the existing dealer, and marks this lead as "Low Priority" in the CRM. This protects your dealer network and avoids internal conflict.
  • Situation B (No Dealer in That Area): The AI treats the customer as a fresh B2B opportunity, shares the complete catalog with pricing, explains dealership benefits, asks about shop size, monthly demand, and stock capacity, and collects proper business details. This becomes a potential expansion lead.

3) Smart Selling & Cross-Selling System

The AI does not just answer questions. If a customer asks for a "Charging cable", the AI responds like a smart salesperson:

  • Suggests compatible chargers
  • Suggests combo offers
  • Suggests higher margin products
  • Suggests bulk discounts

Example: "Sir, along with the cable, many of our dealers also stock fast chargers and power banks for better margin. Would you like to see those options as well?" This increases average order value automatically.

4) Lead Qualification System (Understanding Customer Seriousness)

The AI automatically tracks how fast the customer replies, whether they ask price seriously, whether they ask about bulk quantities, and whether they negotiate. Based on behavior, the AI categorizes leads into:

  • 🔥 Hot Lead – High buying intention
  • 🟡 Warm Lead – Interested but not final
  • ❄️ Cold Lead – Just enquiry

Hot leads are automatically notified to the sales team for faster action.

5) Automated Follow-Up System

If a customer stops replying, the AI takes over follow-ups automatically:

  • After a few hours → Gentle reminder
  • After 24 hours → Friendly follow-up
  • After 48 hours → Soft closing message

Example: "Sir, just checking if you need any help with pricing. I can assist you anytime."
If the customer is already speaking to a human, the AI stays silent and only tracks internally.

6) Human Handoff System

If the customer wants a call, confirms a bulk order, starts serious negotiation, or asks something complex, the AI immediately notifies the assigned sales executive, transfers the chat, and sends a full conversation summary to the executive. This ensures a smooth transition without repeating information.

7) Multi-Language Communication

If a customer messages in Hindi, the AI replies in Hindi. If a customer messages in English, the AI replies in English. This improves trust and comfort for customers from different regions.

8) Backend Tracking & Data Storage

Every single conversation is stored securely in the CRM. This helps in tracking customer history, knowing past discussions, measuring response times, and checking the performance of the AI.

Version 1 – Integrated In-House CRM System (Detailed Explanation)

CRM means Customer Relationship Management. In simple words, it is a digital office where all leads, dealers, orders, and dispatch records are managed in one place. Let’s understand how it works step by step.

1) Lead Dashboard

All new WhatsApp inquiries automatically appear in the CRM dashboard. Each lead contains the Name, Phone number, Location, Interested products, Lead type (Hot/Warm/Cold), and a Conversation summary. The sales team can see everything clearly without checking WhatsApp manually.

2) Lead Pipeline System

Leads move through different stages visually: New Lead → Contacted → Follow-Up → Negotiation → Closed → Converted Dealer.

This helps management understand exactly how many leads are pending, how many are in negotiation, and how many got converted at a single glance.

3) Manual Purchase Entry

When a deal is finalized, the employee enters the Product name, Quantity, Total amount, Dealer name, and Payment status into the CRM. This instantly creates an official purchase record.

4) Dispatch & Delivery Tracking

After purchase entry, the order status moves through: Processing → Packed → Dispatched → Delivered.

When the status changes in the CRM, the customer automatically receives a WhatsApp update. (Example: "Your order has been dispatched and will reach in 2–3 days.") This drastically reduces manual calling.

5) Smart Re-Engagement System

The system intelligently tracks the last purchase date. If a dealer does not reorder, the CRM automatically triggers the AI to reach out:

  • After 15 days → Reminder message
  • After 20 days → Follow-up
  • After 30 days → Stock refill suggestion

This increases repeat business completely automatically.

6) Employee Access System

Up to 10 employees can log in with their ID, view their assigned leads, update statuses, and track their personal performance. Meanwhile, Management can monitor which employee closed how many deals, response times, and total revenue generated.

7) Reports & Basic Analytics (Version 1)

The CRM provides essential business insights including Total leads this month, Conversion rate, Revenue summary, and the Hot vs. Cold lead ratio to help drive better business decisions.

Version 2 – Future Expansion Scope

The system is designed to grow into a full business ecosystem. In Version 2, the CRM and AI can be seamlessly expanded into:

1) Automated Invoice Generation

GST-compliant invoice creation, Auto PDF generation, Direct WhatsApp sending, and Invoice history storage.

2) Quotation Generator System

Select products from the catalog, Add margin percentages, Auto price calculation, Generate professional quotation PDFs, and Send directly to the dealer.

3) Inventory Management System

Stock entry system, Automatic stock deduction on confirmed orders, Low stock alerts, and Fast moving vs. slow moving product analysis.

4) Auto Calling Integration

One-click calling directly from the CRM, Call logging, Conversation notes storage, and Call history tracking.

5) Advanced Analytics Dashboard

Employee performance ranking, Revenue forecasting, Product demand trends, Dealer growth mapping, and Monthly sales growth charts.
